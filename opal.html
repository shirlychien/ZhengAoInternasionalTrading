Opal

Opal is one of the most fascinating gemstones, the rich color in it shows unsurpassed splendor and mystique. Each stone has a different and unique color, even one individual stone can produce various colors depending on the lighting or the way you observe it. Pliny, the famous Roman author, called Opal as “all colors shine and sparkle together in a beautiful combination. It has the fine sparkle of Almandine, the shining purple of Amethyst, the golden yellow of Topaz, and the deep blue of Sapphire.”

Australia is the most important supplier of fine opals which supply almost 95% of opals worldwidely. The most famous type of opal, black opal, is found only in Australia, and it is also rich of boulder opal in this country.
